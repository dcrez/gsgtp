1. Single cylinder, air cooled, naturally aspirated, four cycle, flat head engine.
  2. Maximum engine displacement: Pro Stock: 50.5 cu. in. Super Stock: 30.5 and/or 50.5
  3. Promoter may choose any combination of tractor classes by cu. in.
  4. Pro Stock crankshaft and camshaft must be in original position.
  5. Pro Stock must maintain original measurement from center of crankshaft to top deck of block and original head bolt pattern.
  6. Carburetor size and fuel injection limits by class.
    • Pro Stock: Stock appearing carburetor limited to a maximum air restricting venturi size of 1.200 in. Air flow must be controlled by throttle shaft actuated butterfly in stock location. No air may enter intake after 1.200 in. restriction.
    • Super Stock: Open carburetion and injection limits.
  7. Billet steel or aluminum flywheel and/or starter pulley required.
  8. Engine and driven components must be shielded per requirements in General Rules - Shielding for this class.
  9. Stock block engines bored .040 and over manufacturer's standard specification require a cylinder head restraint devise. Refer to General Rules - Shielding for entire rule.
  10. Maximum tire size: 26x12.00x12.
  11. Maximum wheelbase: 56 inches. Any garden tractor chassis having a stock wheelbase of 56 inches or longer must retain the stock wheelbase for that model tractor.
  12. Maximum distance from center of rear axle to farthest point forward to be 96 inches.
  13. Chassis and transaxle must be from an OEM produced garden tractor. Lengthening of stock chassis allowed provided frame remains stock appearing and the same material type, size, and dimensions as stock are used.
  14. Tractor must have a stock appearing hood and grill (nose piece). Switching to another factory sheet metal is permissible.
  15. Gasoline or alcohol fuel only.
  16. Maximum weight of vehicle and driver: 50.5 cu. in. Pro Stock or Super Stock: 1050 lbs., 30.5 cu. in. Super Stock: 950 lbs.
