All off the lawn classes do not get the first hook option.

  1. You must have wheelie bars if your hitch is above the center line of the axel or you use balance weights or if you have an adjustable hitch.
  2. There are two classes for off the lawn: 900# and 1100#. This is tractor and driver with weights and safety equipment.
  3. All drivers must wear a helmet.
  4. Weights must be secure.
  5. Max tire size allowed is 12x12x24. Turf tires only, bar lug tires must go to stock class and are not allowed on asphalt. Gator tires and 12X12X26 must go to stock class.
  6. Engine RPM must be governor controlled. Maximum rpm will be rated at the maximum horsepower when multiple ratings given. Horse Power Rating:
    • 14 hp and under: 4,000 rpm
    • 16 hp is 3800 rpm
    • 18 h.p. 3600 rpm 20 hp and up is 3400 rpm
    • 23 hp is the max allowed in the class
  7. No fabricated hitches over 11” If you have a stock hitch and the height is higher than 11", than a clevis will be used.
  8. Maximum 2 hooks per tractor
  9. No cutting or syphoning on tires allowed.
