1. Any engine with less than 510 cubic inch.
  2. One carburetor only. Naturally aspirated. Exhaust must be straight up. Fuel must be gas only, no alcohol or nitrous.
  3. Outside size no wider than 6 ft and no longer than 8 ft including weights from center of rear axle to front of tractor. Must have fenders. Two front and two rear engine mounts and one transmission mount.
  4. Shielding: Harmonic balancers, belts and fans must be covered 360 degrees with 1/8 inch steel or ¼-inch aluminum. Engine shields, from front to back, with 1/8 inch steel or ¼ inch aluminum.
  5. Automatic transmissions, drive shaft hoops, and clutch bell housings. Use Sema approved blanket or shielded with 1/8-inch steel or ¼ inch aluminum.
  6. Tire size: 15 X 15.5 X 31
  7. Roll bars/cage 1.25”minimum tubing is required. Lap belt is also required.
  8. Garden tractor appearing sheet metal (hood and grill) is preferred.
