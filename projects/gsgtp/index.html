Schedule a Pull

We try to make scheduling pulls with our club as easy as possible. If you or your town are looking for the kind of entertainment that GSGTP can provide, here are the basic pieces of information that we'll need to review with you:

  1. When: What day and time is your event?
  2. Who: Is there a group or groups that are sponsoring this event?
  3. Fees: We charge to show up; though our team is not-for-profit, working on a fee schedule allows us to purchase/maintain club equipment.
  4. Timelines: Save when you book early!
